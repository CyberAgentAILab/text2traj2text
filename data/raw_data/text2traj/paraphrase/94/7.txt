A diet follower pays close attention to selecting items that match their specific diet, such as keto or paleo. They primarily purchase high-fat foods for a keto diet or unprocessed foods for a paleo diet. By shopping with a predetermined list, they maintain their commitment to their diet. The customer prioritizes finding diet-compatible items over price, often opting for specialty products that fit their diet. They derive satisfaction from cooking meals that cater to their chosen diet, frequently preparing keto-friendly or paleo-friendly dishes.
