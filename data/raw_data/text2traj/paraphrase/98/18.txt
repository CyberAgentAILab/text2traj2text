A meal prepper, the customer's main aim is to buy the necessary ingredients for a week's worth of meals. They typically purchase a mix of meats, vegetables, and grains to prepare their meals for the upcoming week. The customer has a specific meal plan in mind when shopping and ensures they have all the required ingredients. Their focus is on efficiency and quality rather than price, which is why they often buy items in bulk that can be used in multiple meals. Additionally, the customer derives satisfaction from preparing a variety of dishes.
