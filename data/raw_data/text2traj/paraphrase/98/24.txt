When shopping, the customer, who is a meal prepper, concentrates on buying ingredients that will be sufficient for a week's worth of meals. They typically purchase a variety of meats, vegetables, and grains, which they use to prepare their meals for the upcoming week. The customer always has a specific meal plan in mind and ensures they have all the required ingredients. Prioritizing efficiency and quality over price, the customer often buys items in bulk that can be used in multiple meals. Furthermore, the customer takes pleasure in creating a variety of dishes.
